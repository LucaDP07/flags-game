Flags Game



Welcome to the Flags Game!

Before getting started and have some fun, let's take a moment and see what the rules are:
Once the player click "Play" the game board will show a different set of 6 flags every 3 seconds.
5 of these represent the Irish flag, while the last one it's the Italian flag, very similar to the Irish one. The player needs to catch the italian flag (the intruder). Everytime the player clicks on the Italian flag a point will be added to the score, and to make sure the player is paying attention, every click on the Irish flag will take away 2 points. Are you ready? Let's get started!
